Welcome Message

President, Allied Health Practitioners Negeri Terengganu

On behalf of the Allied Health Practitioners Negeri Terengganu, it is my great pleasure to welcome you to the 2nd Terengganu Allied Health Professional Scientific Conference.

This conference provides an important platform for allied health professionals to present research, share clinical experiences, and explore innovations that contribute to better health outcomes.

With the theme "Empowering Allied Health Professional: Leading Change for Better Health Outcomes", this conference aims to bring together professionals from various disciplines to collaborate, learn, and grow together.

We look forward to your participation and meaningful engagement.

Conference Objectives

Networking

Provide a platform for allied health professionals to network, collaborate, and share knowledge across different disciplines and institutions.

Innovation

Showcase innovative research, clinical practices, and technological advancements in allied health fields to improve patient care and outcomes.

Professional Development

Enhance professional competencies through evidence-based learning, workshops, and discussions led by experts in various allied health fields.
